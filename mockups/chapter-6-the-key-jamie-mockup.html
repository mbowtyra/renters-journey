About Jamie

Jamie is a single parent in suburban Philadelphia, but the family support system is in Jersey. Their daughter Emma is starting kindergarten in five months, which means whatever happens next has to happen fast. The plan is Jersey City. The schools are good, the transit is doable, but the budget is tight, and the moment the lease arrives is the moment it all becomes real.

School quality is the first filter, then safe streets and a walkable park. A two-bedroom is non-negotiable. Pet-friendly stays on the list because Emma keeps asking about a small dog.

Jamie reads every review, cross-references school ratings, and lurks in three NJ parent Facebook groups. They want one good place. Not perfect, just good. Emma is a narrative character throughout the journey.
