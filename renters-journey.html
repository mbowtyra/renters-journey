This is dogfooding.

Using the product the way our renters do. The goal is empathy, not quality assurance. The point is to close the gap between what we think the renter experience is and what it actually is.

How the journey works

1

Four personas. Pick whose path you want to walk.

2

Walk seven chapters

From the search to the signing, all the way to the welcome mat. Each chapter has its own surfaces and its own quests.

3

Real product actions on apartmentlist.com. The quiz, the matches, the LDPs, the Renter Hub. Every surface a renter touches.

4

Collect Field Notes

Copyable takeaways you can drop into Slack to share with other A-Listers. Your observations are the data.
